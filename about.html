About Us

Dr. Ka-Wing Chew

"When I started practicing dentistry in the mid-1980's, I made a promise to offer my patients gentle, friendly, and quality dental care. I treated them the same way I treated my family. Nothing has changed over the years, and I am still committed to continue the tradition for as long as I continue to practice dentistry."

Ever since he began practicing dentistry, Dr. Chew has kept up to date on many continuing educational classes, ensuring that his patients receive the most comprehensive and advanced dental care.
