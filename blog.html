COVID-19

We are currently accepting non-emergency patients!

Under specific guidelines by the City and County of San Francisco, our office will accept non-emergency procedures. In compliance with these guidelines we will be selectively choosing appointment dates to ensure one patient at a time will get their procedure done in a timely and safe manner.

We ask all patients to do the following in compliance with the guidelines set by the CDC and the City and County of San Francisco:

  • Wear a face mask except during the procedure.
  • Use the hand sanitizer at the front desk as soon as they come in.
  • Answer the questions specified in the Patient Screening Form.
    • Patient Questionairre
    Do you have a fever or have felt hot/feverish during the past two weeks?

    Have you been experiencing shortness of breath or any other difficulties breathing?

    Do you have a cough?

    Do you have any other flu-like symptoms, such as gastrointestinal upset, headache, or fatigue?

    Have you been experiencing recent loss of taste or smell?

    Have you been in contact with any confirmed COVID-19 positive patients?

    Are you over 60 years old?

    Do you have heart disease, lung disease, kidney disease, diabetes, or any auto-immune disorders?

    Have you been traveling to any regions heavily afflicted by COVID-19?
  • Have their temperature taken by the receptionist.
  • Take a pre-rinse of 1.5% hydro-peroxide provided by the office.


    • If any of the questions are answered "Yes" on the Patient Screening Form, we will ask patients to reschedule.

    Stay safe and wash your hands afterwards,

    Safety during procedures

    Having a procedure done during the time of a pandemic can be worrisome, since patients and Dr. Chew will be in close proximity.

    While patients will be required to remove their face masks during the procedure. Dr. Chew will wear an N-95 face mask or a Respiratory Protection Helmet to ensure the safety of all patients during treatment.

    Special Equipment



    Extra-oral hood suction technology features a triple filtration system to remove aerosols and microbes with a UV-C 200-225nm wavelength lamp for sterilization.

    UV Air Filter

    The GermAwayUV air purifier uses a powerful yet quiet fan to continually cycle all of the air in the room through the system. Once the air goes through, it will be met by a quintuple filtration and sanitation system that culminates in a full 220 watt UV-C germicidal irradiation. This will eliminate bacteria, viruses, and harmful pollutants as quickly as possible without exposing anybody to any UV-C rays.
